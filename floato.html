About Floato

Floato takes you on an exhilarating aerial adventure across Sri Lanka's most breathtaking locations. In this captivating portrait-mode shooter, you'll defend 25 iconic Sri Lankan cities from waves of hostile birds and dangerous enemy aircraft.Begin your journey in Anuradhapura's bustling cityscape and progress through diverse environments including the misty highlands of Nuwara Eliya, ancient temples of Polonnaruwa, pristine beaches of Matara, and the wildlife-rich plains of Monaragala. Each level features meticulously crafted visuals that capture the essence and beauty of Sri Lanka's most treasured destinations.

As threats intensify across your journey, you'll collect and upgrade a variety of birds, each with unique abilities. Deploy swift kingfishers for precision strikes, powerful hornbills for explosive damage, or defensive peacocks to shield your forces. Strategic bird selection becomes crucial as you face increasingly challenging waves of enemies.

The intuitive touch controls make Floato accessible for casual players, while the progressive difficulty curve and strategic depth will satisfy even the most experienced gamers. The immersive soundscape features authentic Sri Lankan musical elements that enhance the cultural experience.Daily challenges and seasonal events offer fresh content and exciting rewards, while global leaderboards let you compete with players worldwide for the title of ultimate Sky Guardian.

Download Floato today and experience the thrill of aerial combat while exploring Sri Lanka's most beautiful locations—one city at a time!
