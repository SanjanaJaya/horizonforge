About Floato

Floato is an immersive action-adventure game that takes players on a breathtaking journey through a world of floating islands suspended in an endless sky. As one of the last descendants of the ancient Floatonians, you must harness the power of the wind to navigate between islands, solve intricate puzzles, and battle mysterious creatures that threaten the balance of your world.

The game features a stunning open world with diverse biomes, from lush floating gardens to treacherous volcanic rocks and crystalline ice formations. Each area presents unique challenges and secrets to uncover as you progress through the compelling narrative.

With an innovative air-gliding mechanic, players can soar through the skies, catch updrafts, and explore the vast expanses between islands. The game's physics-based puzzle system requires creative thinking and mastery of the wind elements to progress.

Floato supports both single-player and co-operative gameplay, allowing friends to explore the world together, combining their abilities to overcome obstacles that would be impossible alone.
