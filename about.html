About Horizon Forge Studios

Our Values

The core principles that guide everything we do

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible in gaming, always seeking new ways to surprise and delight players with fresh ideas and cutting-edge technology.

Community

Our players are at the heart of everything we do. We actively listen to feedback and strive to build meaningful connections with our gaming community.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering the highest quality games possible, with meticulous attention to detail and a refusal to compromise on our creative vision.

Storytelling

We believe in the power of narrative to create meaningful and memorable gaming experiences that resonate with players long after they've put down the controller.

Integrity

We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and respect, treating our employees, partners, and players with fairness and consideration.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable development practices and minimizing our environmental impact while creating positive change in the gaming industry.

Innovative Sri Lankan game studio blending global gaming trends with rich cultural storytelling and distinctive South Asian perspectives.
